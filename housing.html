HOUSING

Homestay Live with a local host or host family on a half-board or self-catering basis. Homestay is a great way of learning more about the life and culture of Edinburgh and to practise everyday English language skills. The cost of Homestay accommodation can vary depending on whether you choose self-catering or half board (dinner and breakfast provided) options. All utility bills are included in the weekly cost. If you are looking for a “home away from home” this could be the perfect choice for you. On-campus accommodation We have two on-campus student residences at our Milton Road and Granton campuses. Our accommodation provides a suitable base for studying at Edinburgh College with great transport links across the city. Milton Road accommodation Private student residences There are private student halls of residence in Edinburgh which are not owned by the College but aimed at students at the city’s universities, as well as the College. The weekly cost is usually higher than homestay accommodation. Most have a good central location with bus connections to the College.
