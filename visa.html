What visa info do Brazilians students need to arrive in Scotland

    • International Vaccination Certificate;

      It always good have a Vaccination certificate, depending on immigration they may ask for it.

    • Proof of Acomodation;

      An invitation letter from a host or a hotel booking is necessary to proof where you will be stating.

    • Travel insurance;

      In many countries, it is mandatory to have travel insurance to emergency medical and repatriation.

    • Valid Passport;

      A valid Passport is very important to travel for another country, it must often be valid for at least six months from the date of entry into the destination.
